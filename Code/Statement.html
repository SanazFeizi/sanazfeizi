Human issues have always held great significance for me. Over the course of my life, I have come to the realization that simply creating forms is not enough to satisfy my creative drive. Consequently, I have made it my mission to infuse my artwork with concepts and meaning, using it as a platform for addressing a range of socio-political and cultural concerns. My ultimate goal is to stimulate reflection among viewers and inspire positive action toward creating a world defined by peace and tranquility. While I address a variety of issues in my work, such as freedom of expression, child marriage, and world peace, I consider women's issues to be at the core of my design practice.
